Hansje is a Dutch-Indonesian actress based in Amsterdam, known for her magnetic presence and instinctive grasp of character. With an adaptable look and experience portraying characters from all layers of society, her performances feel lived-in and authentic.

Trained at the Mulholland Academy and the Dutch Actors School, she has worked across theatre, film, television, and commercial productions in the Netherlands — in both Dutch and English. Physically confident, fluent in four languages, and fully flexible to work anywhere.
